Tenglong Cave (2018.4)

Tenglong Cave is a cave located 6.8 km from Lichuan City, Hubei, China. It is believed to be the longest monomer karst cave system in the world. The cave entrance is 74 m and 64 m wide, leading to 59.8 km of passageways (wikipedia). Together with friends from the hiking club, I took a 3 day hiking in the nearby area.
