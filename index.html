About this site

Hi, my name is Mingyu Wang.

When I began my undergraduate study, I joined the hiking club of my university. From then on, I traveled and hiked all across China with some aweson friends I met in the club. I think of the days in the nature as the shiniest part of my life whenever I look back to them. So I decide to record them using this site.

This is not a photographic exihibition, it's just a record and remenmbrance of the past days.
